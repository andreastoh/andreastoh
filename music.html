Where it all began...

In 2010, a group of three friends - Andreas, Omeigar & Raf, stepped out of their comfort zone from Beat Merchants (a now defunct jamming studio tucked away in a small, three-storey shophouse just off Bugis) to perform their first gig at the Chocolate Bar.

  Singapore

Our staple shows were mainly in our home country where there was a vibrant, growing punk community in the early 2010s. Indoors, outdoors, rooftops & basements, we've played them all.
